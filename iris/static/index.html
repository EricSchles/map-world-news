How it works.

  1. Click any country on the map, search, or click a category to retrieve news stories.
  2. The words of each article are checked to see if the article is positive or negative.
  3. If an article is positive, all the countries mentioned in the article are colored green for good. They are colored red if the article is negative, and blue if it is neutral.

The Project.

The IRIS project was started by Robert Kuykendall, Shensi Ding, Sahil Ansari, and Bo Xu during a Digitally Mediated Storytelling class at Columbia University in 2014.
