How it works.

  1. Select a AP category to retrieve stories.
  2. The words of each article are checked to see if the article is positive or negative, and countries mentioned are extracted.
  3. If an article is positive, all the countries mentioned in the article are colored green for good. They are colored red if the article is negative, and blue if it is neutral.
